ذُو ٱلْجَلَالِ وَٱلْإِكْرَام

Dhu'l-Jalal wa'l-Ikram, meaning "The Possessor of Majesty and Honor," is one of the divine names of Allah. This name signifies Allah's attribute of possessing majestic and honorable qualities. Let us acknowledge His majesty and honor in our worship and daily lives.
